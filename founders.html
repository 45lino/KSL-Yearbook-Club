Our Founders

The visionaries behind KSL Yearbook Club

👨‍💻

Linus Muema

Co-Founder & President

Linus co-founded the KSL Yearbook Club in 2025 with a passion for preserving memories and building community. As a 2025 Advocates Training Programme student, he recognized the need for a platform that would capture and celebrate the unique experiences of KSL students.

His dedication to excellence and vision for creating lasting memories has been instrumental in establishing the club's foundation. Linus believes that every student's story deserves to be told and remembered.

Under his leadership, the club has grown into a vibrant community dedicated to documenting the essence of student life at Kenya School of Law.

👩‍🎨

Sandra Muthoni

Co-Founder & Vice President

Sandra co-founded the KSL Yearbook Club with creativity and enthusiasm, bringing a unique artistic perspective to the club's vision. As a 2025 ATP student, she understands the importance of capturing the moments that make the law school experience special.

Her commitment to inclusivity ensures that every student's voice is heard and every moment is captured. Sandra believes in the power of photography and storytelling to create connections that last beyond graduation.

Through her creative direction, the club produces yearbooks that are not just books, but treasured keepsakes that celebrate the diversity and spirit of the KSL community.
