About Us

Our Story, Vision & Mission

Our Story

The KSL Yearbook Club was founded in 2025 by Linus Muema and Sandra Muthoni - 2025 Advocates Training Programme (ATP) students - who believed that every moment at Kenya School of Law deserved to be remembered. What started as two friends with an idea has grown into a vibrant community dedicated to capturing the essence of student life.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a yearbook that truly represents the diversity, talent, and spirit of our school community. From documenting school life to celebrating cultural events and sporting triumphs, we've made it our mission to ensure that no memory goes unrecorded.

Today, the KSL Yearbook Club stands as a testament to student creativity and collaboration. We continue to evolve, embracing new technologies and storytelling techniques while staying true to our core purpose – preserving the memories that make our time at Kenya School of Law unforgettable.

Our Vision

To be the leading chronicler of student life at Kenya School of Law, creating timeless yearbooks that capture the spirit, diversity, and excellence of every graduating class. We envision a future where every student's journey is beautifully documented and preserved for generations to come.

We strive to set the standard for yearbook excellence in legal education, combining artistic creativity with journalistic integrity to produce publications that our community will treasure forever.

Our Mission

Our mission is to capture, celebrate, and preserve the memories of Kenya School of Law students through exceptional yearbook publications. We are committed to documenting every significant moment, achievement, and experience that defines student life at KSL.

We aim to foster a sense of community and belonging by ensuring every student is represented in our yearbooks. Through professional photography, thoughtful storytelling, and creative design, we transform fleeting moments into lasting memories.

We are dedicated to excellence, inclusivity, and innovation in everything we do, ensuring that each yearbook we create is a true reflection of our vibrant school community.
